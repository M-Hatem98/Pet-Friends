ABOUT THE CLINIC

A Thriving And Burgeoning Skyscraper District In Dubai, Jumeirah Lake Towers, Or JLT, Is Home To Several Wellness Centers, Residents, And A Pet-Friendly Park.

Living in a swanky, happening part of town could not have gotten better, especially since we have a branch of our premium pet care practice located in the area.

Conveniently situated in JLT, our branch is committed to go by our organizationâ€™s mission of providing exceptional and compassionate veterinary care to the furry residents of this vibrant community. As the leading pet care hospital in the UAE, we aim to make our services more accessible to a larger number of people and their pets. Our team of professionals comprising experienced veterinarians and other pet health workers are devoted to the cause of advancing the path of veterinary care in the UAE.

We look forward to being your ultimate partner in pet healthcare. You can reach us at 800-82 or drop by our branch at Fortune Tower, Cluster C, Jumeirah Lake Towers, Dubai.

For more information or to schedule your next appointment, please contact the Modern Vet front desk by calling 800-82 or you can use the WhatsApp button on our website. We are open 24 hours, every day of the week!

Our Modern Vet JLT is located near Fortune Tower, Lake level, Al Sarayat St, Cluster C, Jumeirah Lake Towers in just a few minutes of walking distance from Palm Jumeirah, and you can drive here within 9 minutes of driving distance via Shiekh Zayed rd interchange no five toward Al Sarayat st.
