PET RELOCATION

Modern Vet, in partnership with Blue Sky Pet Relocation, proudly offers a comprehensive and personalized pet travel service tailored to the unique needs of each pet. Our collaborative approach combines Modern Vet’s veterinary expertise with Blue Sky’s relocation proficiency, ensuring a seamless transition for your beloved pet, whether you’re moving into or out of the UAE.

Transport Of Pets. Import And Export.

We offer the following pet relocation services in Dubai:

  • Specialized Company Transport: This can include vehicles equipped for transporting animals such as cats and dogs, or special containers for shipping on cargo vessels. This service ensures that your pet’s safety and comfort are prioritized during the move.
  • Accompanied in the Passenger Cabin: For those who prefer to keep their pets close, we offer the option of having your pet accompany you in the passenger cabin of an airplane or train. In this case, your pet must be placed in a special carrier that meets the requirements of the airline or railway company. This option is ideal for ensuring that your pet remains calm and secure, surrounded by familiar faces.
  • Cargo Ship Container: This method of transportation is suitable for pets that can endure longer periods without access to fresh air and water. It’s an efficient way to relocate larger animals or pets that do not easily adapt to changes in environment.
  • Van Transport: This option is ideal for small groups of pets and can be more economical than other modes of transportation. It allows for direct and continuous supervision of your pets, ensuring their well-being throughout the journey.

For each type of transportation, our specialists will develop a personalized plan that takes into account all the characteristics of your pet, ensuring their safety and comfort during the relocation process. Our pet relocation Dubai are designed to cater to the needs of your beloved cat or dog, providing a stress-free transition to their new home. We also offer competitive costs for all our services, including Emirates pet relocation, making it easier for you to plan your pet’s move without worrying about the financial aspect.

Modern Vet’s Customized Pet Travel Service

At Modern Vet, we begin with a detailed pet travel assessment and preparation. Our veterinarians gather information about your travel plans and your pet’s medical history to formulate a custom travel plan and timeline. We take into account evolving pet travel regulations and provide guidance on the best practices for a safe journey.

Key aspects of our service include:

  • Travel Planning and Documentation: Development of a custom travel plan, including route options, pricing, and information on travel restrictions.
  • Export Documentation: We provide all necessary export documents such as a valid rabies certificate, export permit, and an official health certificate from our veterinarians.
  • Paperwork and Coordination: Handling all paperwork and liaising with travel partners for a hassle-free experience.
  • Age and Health Requirements: Ensuring pets meet age and health criteria for travel, including vaccinations and microchipping.
  • Travel Tips and Acclimation Guidance: Offering advice for travel preparation and helping pets acclimate to their new home. Blue Sky Pet Relocation’s Expertise

Blue Sky Pet Relocation brings specialized knowledge in pet moving services, managing the logistics of pet travel with empathy and professionalism. Their team ensures that each pet’s journey is comfortable, safe, and stress-free.

Why Choose Our Combined Service?

  • Holistic Approach: Combining Modern Vet’s medical expertise and Blue Sky’s logistical excellence.
  • Personalization: Services tailored to meet the specific needs of your pet.
  • Stress-Free Experience: Ensuring a smooth and worry-free relocation process for both pets and owners.
  • Comprehensive Care: From health checks and documentation to travel booking and acclimation advice.

Modern Vet and Blue Sky Pet Relocation are dedicated to providing a seamless, safe, and caring travel experience for your pet. Trust us to handle every detail of your pet’s relocation with the utmost attention and professionalism.
