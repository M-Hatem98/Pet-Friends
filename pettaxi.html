PET TAXI

In Dubai, our unique Pet Taxi service is tailored exclusively for our furry friends, ensuring they travel directly from and to our clinics with ease and comfort. Unlike typical taxis for humans, our vehicles are crafted specifically for pet transport, prioritizing their safety and well-being.

How Our Pet Taxi Operates:

Our vans are fitted with secure crates, ensuring your pets are safe during transit. A trained veterinary technician or professional animal handler accompanies them, guaranteeing top-tier care. We pride ourselves on being Dubai’s premier pet transport service, focusing on safety, reliability, and excellence.

Frequently Asked Questions:

What is Pet Taxi Dubai?

Our service specifically transports pets between your location in Dubai and our clinics.

Cost and Booking:

A one-way trip costs 89 AED. Book easily using our online system. Cancellations should be made 24 hours in advance to avoid charges, unless in exceptional circumstances.

Vaccination Requirements:

Pets must be vaccinated at least ten days prior to travel, with a rabies tag. We offer free tagging if needed.

Types of Pets Accepted:

We welcome dogs, cats, rabbits, birds, and ferrets under 10 pounds.

Pet Security in Vehicles:

Pets are securely fastened in cages within the vehicle, ensuring their safety.

Pet Pickup Protocol:

The pet owner must be present to sign a release form. Pets should be healthy, non-aggressive, and either leashed or in carriers.

Preparation Tips for Your Trip:

  • Ensure your pet has had a bathroom break before pickup.
  • Limit food intake pre-trip, but feel free to bring familiar items for comfort.
  • Communicate any specific needs or comforting phrases your pet responds to.
  • Special Needs Accommodations:
  • We cater to pets with special requirements, offering various service options for a comfortable journey.

Safety and Security Measures:

Our fleet is meticulously maintained, and drivers are thoroughly vetted, including background checks. We’re committed to ensuring both you and your pet’s safety during transport.

Traveling with Other Pets:

Pets are grouped by species for travel – cats with cats, dogs with dogs, ensuring a stress-free journey.

For any assistance, our customer service team is available 24/7 at 800-82. Book your pet’s safe and specialized journey with us, and experience the best in pet transportation in Dubai.
